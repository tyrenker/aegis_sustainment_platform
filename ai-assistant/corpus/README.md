# Corpus — Phase 8, step 1

Write your own 12-20 short fake maintenance/logistics SOP documents here (`.txt` files) for the
AI assistant to ingest.

Writing these yourself matters directly in Phase 10: you'll plant a hidden prompt injection in
one of these documents later, and you need to know exactly what's in your own corpus to design a
query that plausibly retrieves the poisoned one.

Don't look at `../../aegis-reference/ai-assistant/corpus/` until after you've written your own set
and done your own Phase 10 attack attempt — it's there for comparison afterward, not as a
starting point.
